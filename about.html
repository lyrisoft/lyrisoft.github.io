About Us

- Our Story -

Lyrisoft  is a software company based in Abuja, Nigeria. Founded in 2017 by a group of passionate developers, programmers and designers.
Everyone at Lyrisoft is free to live and work whereever they want. Many of us love working remotely.

We're programmers, designers, thinkers, Innovators, filmmakers, software engineers and hard workers. With such diverse group of people, from so many different places, we bring a unique touch and perspective to everything we do. Get in touch with us today!


- Our Mission -

  • To provide the best possible products and services to our clients at all times and no matter what irrespective of their geo-location.
  • To be the best at the products and services we offer.

- Our Values -

  • TRUST- at Lyrisoft, we do everything within our capacity to always satisfy our growing clients who trust us enough to get the job done for them.
  • EFFICIENT & RELIABLE - we are young and vibrant and are ready to take any challenge thrown at us.
  • ON-TIME DELIVERY- we go beyond ourselves to make sure that our trusted clients get the product and services the paid for and on time.

- Our Vision -

  • To be a leading software company recognized both nationally and internationally.
  • To run an internationally recognized social network for up-coming programmers and existing ones, where they would share, get help, and contribute code to the community.
  • To provide products and services to clients with recognized and acceptable standards by leveraging on available tech tools.
  • To provide products and services to clients irrespective of their geo-location.
